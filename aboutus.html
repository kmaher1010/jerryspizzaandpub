Jerry’s Pizza and Pub is a family friendly neighborhood restaurant located in Chicago, IL since 1999. We serve a variety of food including pizza, burgers, sandwiches, and a full bar. We are open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We offer dine-in, takeout, delivery, and catering.

We have a seating capacity of 200 including a private room for parties with a capacity of 100. We have room for 30 at our full-service bar. Live music is scheduled on the weekends. We currently include 30 different beers on tap from a variety of local venders. On your next night out, visit Jerry’s for a great meal, entertainment, and a fantastic night out. We look forward to serving you.

Jerry's mission is to serve our customers high quality food with a family friendly neighborhood atmosphere. We believe our high standards and values will is what define us.

Jerry's was founded in 1999 by Jerry and his family. Jerry's is a family owned restaurant.
